About Us

Laroy Aggregate Company has been a leading supplier of high-quality sand, gravel, and other construction materials in Dodoma for over a decade. We take pride in our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, offering reliable services to the construction industry.

Company Overview

Established in 2010, Laroy Aggregate Company has become a cornerstone in Dodoma’s construction industry. We provide premium aggregates for infrastructure, residential, and commercial projects. With a dedicated team and state-of-the-art equipment, we ensure the best products and services for our clients.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leading provider of sustainable and high-quality construction materials in Tanzania, shaping the future of infrastructure development through innovation, efficiency, and reliability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide superior aggregate products that meet the needs of our clients while ensuring environmental sustainability, community development, and the growth of the construction sector.
